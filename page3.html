Religion and Literature

As a research assistant for the Wooster religious studies department, I compiled a list of sources on the relationship between literary theory and religious studies via College of Wooster library databases and other resources. My research focused the relationship between religion and literature in contemporary academics (1950 to present). I read many academic sources on religion and literature and examined various perspectives on the relationship between religion and literature and the overlap between these two fields of study. I also looked at some particular contemporary examples of authors of "religious fiction" such as Yann Martel and Flannery O'Connor.
